## Target Market

### Primary Segments

**1. Small & Medium Businesses (10–200 employees)**
- HR managers drowning in spreadsheets
- No budget for enterprise HRMS like SAP or Workday
- Need simple, fast, affordable workforce management

**2. Staffing & Recruitment Agencies**
- Managing 50–500 contractors across multiple clients
- Need task tracking and payroll proof
- Web3 payroll verification is a strong differentiator

**3. Remote-First Tech Startups**
- Distributed teams across time zones
- Need AI-assisted task assignment
- Web3-native founders appreciate blockchain payroll logging

## 3-Month GTM Roadmap — First 50–100 Companies

### Month 1 — Foundation & Early Adopters (0 → 15 companies)

**Week 1–2: Product Hunt & Communities**
- Launch on Product Hunt with demo video
- Post in Reddit communities: r/entrepreneur, r/startups, r/humanresources
- LinkedIn posts targeting HR managers in India and SEA
- Target: 500 signups, 15 paying companies

**Week 3–4: Direct Outreach**
- Cold email 200 HR managers and founders
- Offer free 3-month Pro trial for first 20 companies
- Collect feedback and testimonials
- Refine onboarding flow based on feedback

### Month 2 — Community & Partnerships (15 → 50 companies)

**Startup Communities**
- Partner with 5 startup incubators (IIT incubators, T-Hub, NASSCOM)
- Offer incubatee companies free plan
- Get featured in their newsletters

**Content Marketing**
- Publish 2 blog posts per week on HR automation and AI workforce management
- YouTube tutorial: "How to manage your team with AI"
- SEO target keywords: "AI HRMS India", "free HR management tool"

**Referral Program**
- Existing customers get 1 month free for every referral
- Target: each customer refers 2 others

### Month 3 — Scale & Retention (50 → 100 companies)

**Paid Acquisition**
- Run targeted LinkedIn ads to HR Managers and Founders
- Google Ads on keywords: "employee task management software"
- Retargeting campaigns for website visitors

**Product-Led Growth**
- Free tier allows up to 5 employees (viral loop)
- Companies invite employees → employees see the product → company upgrades
- In-app referral prompts after key actions
